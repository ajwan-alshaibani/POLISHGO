# AI-Driven Cleaning Platform (Polish & Go)

## Overview
Developed an AI-driven on-demand cleaning platform to improve efficiency, affordability, and service quality in the residential cleaning industry.

## Key Features
- Multi-sided platform connecting service providers ("Polishers") with customers
- Automated dispatch system to reduce wait times
- AI-based quality control using checklists and verification
- Real-time service tracking and transparency features

## Business Model
- Commission-based revenue on transactions
- Subscription model for premium users
- Focus on scalability and cost-efficiency

## Financial Analysis
- Built financial model including pricing strategy and subscription tiers
- Estimated development and operational costs (~$60K+)
- Evaluated profitability and scalability of the platform

## Market Opportunity
- Targeted a ~$60B cleaning services market with high unmet demand
- Focused on affordability and accessibility for mass adoption
